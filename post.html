Game 2 of the World Series this year was a BLAST!

Not only did the Dodgers win, but I also got to spend the day with family at the game.

My Favorite Parts of the Game

  1. The Environment
    • There is nothing quite like watching the Dodgers beat the Yankees while chewing sunflower seeds and eating peanuts.
    • The stadium felt alive as we stood for the majority of the game.
    • The tension between Dodger fans and Yankees fans was high, only creating adrenaline for us all.
  2. Family
    • My wife's first ever playoff game was a success, and she was smiling the whole time. Especially after we got her a shirt!
    • Celebrating with my dad brought back memories of high school and biting our nails on the couch as we watched the Dodgers.
